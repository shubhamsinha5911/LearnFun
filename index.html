Standards We Teach

Class 10th

In the 10th grade, students typically deepen their understanding of core subjects like Math, Science, Language Arts, and Social Studies.
This year marks a crucial phase in academic growth, as students prepare for standardized assessments and explore potential career interests.This year is significant as students prepare for college entrance exams, explore higher-level coursework.

Class 11th

In the 11th grade, students usually continue to advance their knowledge in core subjects such as Mathematics, Science, Language Arts, and Social Studies,
often with a more specialized or in-depth approach. This year is significant as students prepare for college entrance exams, explore higher-level coursework, and consider potential career paths.

Class 12th

In the 12th grade, students typically finalize their high school education by further specializing in chosen subjects, preparing for college applications or vocational paths,
and often taking advanced courses or exams. This year is crucial for college admissions, scholarship applications, and making decisions about future academic or career paths
